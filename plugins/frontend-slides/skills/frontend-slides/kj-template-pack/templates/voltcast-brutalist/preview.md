## Visual Snapshot

A high-voltage brutalist hardware-spec system — acid-green on near-black void, built like a synth front panel. Display is Archivo Black, a single-weight ultra-heavy grotesque set in screaming uppercase at poster scale with tight negative tracking and zero rotation; authority comes from sheer mass. Every label and the body run JetBrains Mono — paragraphs in weight 400 muted gray, and metadata in weight 700 uppercase that always opens with a `// ` code-comment prefix. The palette is uncompromising single-accent: void `#0B0B0F`, one saturated acid green `#C6FF00`, off-white bone `#EDEDE6` text, muted gray `#8A8A82` body, and a dark wire gray `#2A2A33` for hairline grid rules.

There are zero rounded corners, zero drop shadows, and zero gradients. Depth is purely structural: a persistent 3px acid top-bar and 6px acid bottom progress-rule frame every slide, hard wire-thin grid rules (3px outer + 1px inner) separate cells, and the one big move is surface inversion — a full-bleed acid panel flips the whole surface to void-on-acid for the stat moment. The register is loud, technical, and Berlin-industrial — closer to a Eurorack module's silkscreen than a corporate slide.

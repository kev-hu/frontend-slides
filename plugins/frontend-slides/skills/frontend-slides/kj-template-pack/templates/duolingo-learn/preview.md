## Visual Snapshot

A bright, gamified deck that feels like opening the Duolingo app's home screen — recomposed into slides, never cloning the app chrome. The whole identity rests on one face, Nunito (600/700/800), with a single non-negotiable rule: every display headline is lowercase, rounded, and friendly. Feather Green (#58CC02) is the unmistakable hero — primary buttons, the logo, the giant hero stat figure, the progress bar, and the one full-bleed inverted panel. Around it sits a labeled gamification accent system where each color carries meaning: bee yellow = XP/crowns, fox orange = streaks, macaw blue = Super/info, cardinal red = hearts/errors, beetle purple = accent.

Depth is chunky and physical: the signature move is the 3D pressable button — fully pill-rounded with a colored solid bottom shadow (`0 7px 0`) that compresses (to `0 3px 0` plus a downward translate) when pressed, so the button literally depresses. The second signature is the circular gamified lesson node — a white-ringed gradient disc with its own chunky `0 8px 0` bottom shadow and an optional level badge — recomposed into a winding-path agenda (zigzag numbered nodes joined by green diagonals), a numbered process step-flow, a section marker, and a horizontal node-path closer. Content runs on green-checkmark bullets and red/green comparison cards; data shows as a multi-stat metrics row. Cards are soft 24px-radius rectangles with a faint lift; progress bars and stat pills are fully pill-rounded. Nothing has a sharp corner. It reads as a real presentation talk — a titled cover with presenter + date, a thank-you close — never a marketing landing page. Surfaces stay bright (white, a barely-grey Polar, and the full-bleed green panel), and gamification emoji (🔥💎❤️👑🦉⭐) supply the icon vocabulary.

## International / CJK Preview Note

Swap Nunito for a rounded CJK face (ZCOOL KuaiLe, or Noto Sans SC 900) for headlines and Noto Sans SC 500 for body. CJK has no case, so the lowercase-headline signature becomes "rounded, casual, heavy" — lean on the rounded face + Feather Green + node motifs to carry the playful register. Set letter-spacing to 0, drop uppercase on buttons/eyebrows, open line-height, and use full-width punctuation with 盘古之白 spacing.

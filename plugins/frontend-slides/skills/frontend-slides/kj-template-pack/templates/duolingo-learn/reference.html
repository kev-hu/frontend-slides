UNIT 01 · THE PROBLEM

the retention problem.

people loved their first lesson — and then most never came back. acquisition wasn't the ceiling. the second day was.

03 / 11
WHAT WE FOUND

five signals in the drop-off.

✓
the first win came too late

learners hit a reward only after a full lesson — minutes before most of them had already left.

✓
nothing tracked momentum

there was no visible streak, so a missed day felt like nothing was lost.

✓
progress was invisible

without a path to see, "how far am i?" had no answer — and no pull forward.

✓
reminders felt like spam

generic push notifications got muted in the first week and never recovered.

✓
effort had no stakes

there was nothing to protect — no hearts, no streak — so quitting cost the learner nothing.

⭐ THE RECAP

what we learned.

✓
make the first win early

a reward in the first 30 seconds beats a bigger reward at the end no one reaches.

✓
give people something to protect

a visible streak turns "i'll skip today" into "i can't break this now."

✓
consistency beat intensity

short daily reps compounded further than any long-session push we tried.

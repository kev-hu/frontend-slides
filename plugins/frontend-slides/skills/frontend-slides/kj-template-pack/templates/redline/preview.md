## Visual Snapshot

A pure-white Swiss canvas with a single hot-red (`#ff3300`) doing every accent. Oversized Archivo Black (900) headlines — tightly tracked, near-lowercase — dominate the upper slide in near-black ink, never red. Above each title sits one short fat red rule-bar (120×12, hard corners). Eyebrows are JetBrains Mono, uppercase, in the `NN / LABEL` form with the number in red. Thin 1px hairline verticals form a quiet Swiss grid behind everything; cards and stat bands are defined by hard 2px black borders with zero rounded corners and no shadows. Body copy is Nunito in gray; large numerals are red Archivo 900. The whole thing reads as engineered and editorial — Bauhaus poster crossed with a technical spec sheet.
